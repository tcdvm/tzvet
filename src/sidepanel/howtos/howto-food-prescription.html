Food Prescriptions

  1. Within the Diagnostics and Treatments tab of the medical records, go to the +medication section to start a new prescription.
  2. Within the medication prescription window, add an externally dispensed prescription under dispense type
  3. Type "external prescription" within the drug name box
  4. Delete the text that’s in the “instructions” section, type .foodrx and hit enter.
  5. Fill out the remaining information and add the prescription to the patient’s records.
