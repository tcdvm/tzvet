Cardiology Consult

  1. Double LEFT click on the patient you need a consult on.
  2. Click on "+Diagnostic/Consult Request"
  3. Under "Veterinarian": enter your clinician requesting the consult
  4. Under "Diagnostic Supplier": enter CVM Cardiology (or 508502)
  5. Under "Diagnostics" enter: SA General Consultation (this is the only type of consult you need to enter, ever! You do not need to enter anything else either.)
  6. Under "Diagnostic Request Specifics/History" enter: some info for the consult and a weight in kg.
