Hospital Policies

Required parts of the clinical record (Initial and Recheck):

  • Master Problem (Initial, New Diagnosis)
  • History
  • Physical Exam
  • Assessment
  • Plan
  • SOAP Notes (if inpatient)
  • Informed Consent (if applicable)
  • Anesthesia & Recovery record (only general anesthesia)
  • Operative Report (if applicable)
  • Discharge Summary
  • CVM Consent – Euthanasia (if applicable)
  • CVM Consent – DOA Necropsy Request (if applicable)

Discharge Summary:

  • Discharge Summary mandatory for every clinical record hospital wide minus Large Animal Field Services, Farrier and Knoxville Zoo
  • Discharge Summary is required at time of discharge
  • Discharge Summary must be entered into the Discharge Summary section of the clinical record
  • Owner signature is no longer needed
